🧘 5 Morning Yoga Poses to Start Your Day

By Krish | June 9, 2025

Boost your energy and mood with these simple morning yoga poses. Start your day on a peaceful note...

🌅 1. Cat-Cow Stretch (Marjaryasana-Bitilasana)
How to do it:
Start on your hands and knees.
Inhale: Arch your back, lift your head and tailbone (Cow Pose).
Exhale: Round your spine, tuck your chin and pelvis (Cat Pose).
Benefits: Warms up the spine, improves flexibility, relieves tension.



🌞 2. Downward-Facing Dog (Adho Mukha Svanasana)
How to do it:
From a tabletop position, lift your hips up and back, forming an inverted “V”.
Keep hands and feet grounded.
Benefits: Stretches the whole body, energizes the system, strengthens arms and legs.



🌻 3. Sun Salutation (Surya Namaskar)
How to do it:
A sequence of 12 flowing poses including forward folds, lunges, and gentle backbends.
Synchronize breath with movement.
Benefits: Stimulates blood flow, warms up muscles, enhances focus and vitality.



🌿 4. Cobra Pose (Bhujangasana)
How to do it:
Lie on your stomach, hands under shoulders.
Inhale and lift your chest without lifting your pelvis.
Benefits: Opens the chest and shoulders, strengthens the spine, boosts mood.



🌺 5. Child’s Pose (Balasana)
How to do it:
Kneel on the mat, sit back on heels, and stretch your arms forward.
Rest your forehead on the ground.
Benefits: Calms the mind, relieves stress, gently stretches the back and hips.



🌈 Tip: Practice these poses for 10–15 minutes every morning with deep breathing to feel refreshed, focused, and ready for the day ahead.
